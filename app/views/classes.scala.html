Arty Monkeys

Ages 18 months - 5 years

These fun and varied classes have a different theme each week with a mix of arts and crafts activities to keep your little monkey interested. We experiment with paint, play dough, rollers, straws, vegetables and much more! Designed by a primary teacher, these classes will help develop your child’s social and cognitive skills. We recommend this class for children aged 18 months to 5 years.

What does the price include?

Our aim is to provide our arty monkeys with as many different art and craft experiences as possible, using a wide range of materials. We will supply waterproof aprons to protect their clothing and all our products are child friendly.

Every week your child will take home their masterpieces!
